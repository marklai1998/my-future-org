How we work

Duration 4 weeks
Allowance Interns will be provided with an allowance as determined by the corporate partners
Eligibility
  1. Students aged 17-18
  2. Self-driven, mature and willing to learn and grow from the experience
  3. A strong interest in applying their knowledge and academic experiences to a work setting
  4. Selected on the basis of certain criteria agreed among their schools and participating corporate partners
Program Timeline February Announcement of "My Future" opportunities
March - April
  1. Interviews by a panel comprising the project team, participating schools and participating corporate partners
  2. Mentor recruitment
May
  1. Result announcement
  2. Students will be matched, as much as possible, with available posts according to their preference
  3. Pre-internship briefing session entailing interns' job duties, responsibilities and requirements
June Students at the corporate partners' permises
July Students debrief and feedback
