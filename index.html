Meaningful summer work life experience
- Engage students in real life working environment
- Broaden exposure
- Learn from supervisor and mentor
In mid-2016, a group of professionals considered it necessary to find ways to help young people aged 16-18 to understand our ever-changing society as part of their preparation for a working life before their completion of secondary education.

The Organising Committee comprises of experienced people from diverse professions: social work, law enforcement, law, finance, government, entertainment, education and property development.

Supporting charitable organisations include Cambridge Hughes Hall Alumni Association, Equilibrium Cultural Education Foundation, Junior Achievement (JA), HK Chapter and Gideon Centre.
